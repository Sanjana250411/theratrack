# 🌿 Panchakarma Patient Management & Therapy Scheduling System

A digital healthcare platform designed to help **Ayurvedic hospitals and Panchakarma centers** manage patient records, therapy schedules, and treatment progress efficiently.

This system replaces traditional **paper-based records** with a modern digital solution to improve coordination between doctors, therapists, and patients.

---

## ✨ Key Features

👨‍⚕️ **Patient Management**
- Register and store patient information
- Maintain medical history and treatment records

📅 **Therapy Scheduling**
- Schedule Panchakarma therapies
- Assign therapy sessions and treatment dates

📊 **Treatment Tracking**
- Monitor patient recovery and therapy progress

🔔 **Notifications & Reminders**
- Therapy session alerts
- Follow-up reminders for patients

📈 **Reports & Analytics**
- Track treatment outcomes
- Generate therapy progress reports
